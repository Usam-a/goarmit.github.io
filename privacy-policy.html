Section 1

Introduction

MyARMIT ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application ("Application").

Section 2

Information We Collect

To provide a secure and functional experience, we collect the following categories of data:

  • Personal information — Name and email address, used for account creation and management.
  • Financial information — Payment details and purchase history, processed securely via Stripe. We do not store raw card numbers.
  • Device identifiers — Unique device or other IDs (such as Android ID or Advertising ID), shared with trusted third-party providers for fraud prevention, app functionality, and analytics.
  • Usage data — Pages visited, time spent in the app, operating system, and IP address, used for analytics and performance improvement.
  • Location data — Approximate geographical location, used for personalized content and analytics.
Section 3

Third-Party Service Providers

We share data with the following trusted third parties to operate the app:

  • Stripe — Payment processing and fraud detection.
  • Pusher — Real-time notifications and data updates.
  • Expo — Application framework and over-the-air updates.
  • Google Play Services — Core app infrastructure.

These providers have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

Section 4

How We Use Your Data

  • App functionality and account management
  • Processing payments and preventing fraud
  • Delivering real-time features and notifications
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Complying with legal obligations
Section 5

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You have the right to request deletion of your personal data at any time.

To request account and data deletion, contact us at asif@jove1.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe and remove your data from our systems.

Section 6

Security

We use industry-standard HTTPS encryption to protect your data in transit. We apply physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect information we process and maintain.

Section 7

Children

The Application is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

Contact us at asif@jove1.com if you have concerns.

Section 8

Opt-Out

You may stop all data collection by uninstalling the Application using the standard uninstall process available on your device or the Google Play Store.

Section 9

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating this page. Continued use of the Application after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
